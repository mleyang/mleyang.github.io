Projects

太空狐狸 SpaceFox Unity VR Project (2015)

During my final quarter at UCLA I made a space exploration game featuring a fox character which I designed and made. Given my strong interest and curiosity for VR, I created a world in Unity and configured it for the Oculus to be explored in first person perspective. The player can explore the planet while collecting different gemstones. I chose a low-poly aesthetic as a style choice as well as to help with optimization for real-time rendering.

About Emily Yang

I am a designer and technical director with experience in feature film vfx but currently working on animated features. My work mainly focuses on 3d computer graphics and interactive coding, but I have a diverse skill set in other elements of design as well.

I was born in Taipei, Taiwan but spent most of my childhood growing up in Vancouver, Canada. I then moved and graduated with a BA in Design Media Arts from UCLA. I enjoy traveling, whale watching, and eating hummus.

I can be reached at emily.yang@ucla.edu, say hi!

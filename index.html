Reel Breakdown

1 Slight Night Shiver (2014)

Programs used: Maya, Mental Ray, RealFlow, Adobe Photoshop, Nuke, Adobe After Effects

I am responsible for all aspects of this project. I wanted to raise awareness for marine life by creating a certain mood for my audience. I painted the texture of the whale in Photoshop. The animation for the jellyfish was done using expressions, and the transparency was achieved by using the sampler info node. I had difficulty getting caustics to look natural being projected onto my whale, but I resolved this by generating caustics using noise patterns in Nuke and connecting the image sequence to the color attribute in the lights in Maya.

2 Forever Alloon (2013)

Programs used: Maya, Mental Ray, Nuke, After Effects, Zbrush

This is my first attempt at making a 3D short after self-teaching Maya. It is a self-initiated short film that I wrote, directed, and created in my own time. The story is about a balloon that is not as brightly colored as the others but longs for a human companion. The main balloon character and its expressions were modeled using Zbrush then imported into Maya. Everything else was modeled in Maya. The string simulation was done using Mayaâ€™s nCloth and the grass was done with fur simulation and shaded with green tones.
